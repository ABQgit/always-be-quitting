Six verbatim ABQ Tips emails (Jon's real sends), each ~2–3 minutes:

- **Welcome: Here's What to Expect from ABQ Tips** — Sets expectations; reframes quitting from dread to something hopeful and doable.
- **One small thing that moves quitting forward** — Small consistent actions build momentum; "do SOMETHING today."
- **What's REALLY stopping you from quitting?** — Three-question self-diagnosis exercise to name the real barriers.
- **Who's afraid of a few withdrawal symptoms? (hint: everyone)** — Countdown to a top-5 withdrawal-symptoms video; #5 sleep disturbances.
